Something is off

You and your friend Michael are wandering through the city. Both of you have your guard up and filled with a sinking feeling that you're being followed.

And then you see it, or should you say him. He's wearing a hood, but you both still know who he is and what he intends to do. Your mind is filled with one thought. Don't let him catch you.

You and Michael are met with a choice. An alleyway or a crowded street.
