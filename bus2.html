Bus Stop

You and Michael pull out your clipper cards and get on the bus as soon as the doors open.

As the two of you walk past, the driver sniffs the air and makes a face.

"Hold it!" You hear him call, "You both reek too much. You'll have to get off"

Defeated, you and Michael get off, and you see your pursuer paces away.

He only needs one of you. Do you sacrifice yourself, or shove Michael towards him?
