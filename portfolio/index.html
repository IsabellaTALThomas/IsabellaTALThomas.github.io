Hello! My name is Isabella Thomas, and this is my Art 74 portfolio at SJSU. Most of my artistic background is in traditional and illustrative media, so working with digital art forms and coding-based projects challenged me to approach creativity in entirely new ways. Throughout this course, I experimented with glitch art, interactive media, 3D design, and web-based artwork while learning the technical processes behind them. Because I was unfamiliar with many of the programs and coding concepts used in class, creating these projects often felt like learning a new language. This portfolio documents both the finished works and my growth in adapting traditional artistic ideas into digital spaces.
