Bathroom

You decide against purchasing a disguise. After all, you have all the clothes you need at home. Sure some have holes and are worn out, but...in this economy?!

Instead, you slip into the bathroom and hide in a stall. You'll never get found in here.

After a few moments, you hear another person enter the bathroom. He paces in front of the stalls slowly until he stops at yours...

"Busted," you hear your friend Jerry say. You sign, exit the stall, and accept your fate.

"Tag!"

Try again?
